The Food and Agriculture Organization estimates that around every 3.5 seconds, someone dies of hunger. That adds up to 25,000 people that lose their lives every day as a result of hunger. That adds up to roughly 9.1 million people who die of starvation each year.
According to unicef, EVERYONE in industrialized countries have access to safe water while in developing countries, only 86% do. Even worse, 39% of people in sub-Saharan Africa STILL do not have access to safe water.
